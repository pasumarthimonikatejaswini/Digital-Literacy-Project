# 📱 Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

# ✅ Do's
- Use social media to **share academic achievements** and professional updates .  
- **Respect privacy**: adjust your settings and be mindful of what you post .  
- **Engage positively**: comment, like, and share content that adds value .  
- **Network professionally**: connect with peers, professors, and industry experts .  
- **Think before posting**: ensure your content reflects well on you as a student .  

# ❌ Don'ts
- Don’t post **offensive or disrespectful content** .  
- Don’t overshare **personal or sensitive information** .  
- Don’t engage in **cyberbullying or negative arguments** .  
- Don’t rely on social media during **study hours or exams** .  
- Don’t spread **misinformation or unverified news** .
