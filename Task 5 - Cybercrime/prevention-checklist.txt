# Stay Safe Online Checklist for College Students

✅ General Safety
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account and update them regularly.  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.  
- Verify links before clicking — hover over them to check the actual URL.  
- Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security patches.  
- Avoid sharing personal details (like Aadhaar, college ID, or address) on public platforms.  
- Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers.  

💳 Financial / UPI Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking passwords with anyone.  
- Only use official UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM) downloaded from trusted sources.  

📢 Reporting Channels
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)  
- **Helpline:** Dial **1930** for immediate assistance.
